H-1B-safe entrepreneurship: ownership without unauthorized work.

The program is engineered around strict H-1B rules: you can own a U.S. company and receive profit, but you cannot “work” in that business. We respect that line completely.

USCIS-aligned design Ownership, not employment
  • H-1B students can legally own 100% of an LLC and receive passive profit distributions.
  • They cannot operate the business: no customer calls, no Shopify ops, no ad management, no daily tasks.
